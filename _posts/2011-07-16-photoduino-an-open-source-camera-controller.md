    Photoduinois an open source camera controller based on theArduino platform.It is designed to take photographs using various techniques such as time-lapses, high speed photography, photography of
    animals, and storms photography.The electronic circuit is capable to control autofocus and shutter of a camera and two separate manuals flashes.It also has a sound sensor, an impact/shock sensor,
    a laser barrier and an infrared barrier that can be used to take pictures when an event is registered.The system is currently compatible withmultitude of camerasbut may be adaptable in the future
    to other models and brands that have remote shutter connector cable.
