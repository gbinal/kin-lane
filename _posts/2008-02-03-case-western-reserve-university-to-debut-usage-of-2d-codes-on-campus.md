{% include JB/setup %}<p>I was turned to an article about usage of QR Codes on Campus over at 2d-code.co.uk.Beginning February 1st 2008, students, faculty and staff at Case Western Reserves Cleveland, Ohio, campus will be participating in the nations first trial of 2D codes—commonly referred to as QR Codes—by bringing the cell phone technology to campus.The 2D codes, first introduced on campus during fall orientation will be used as a link to mobile content in the form of pictures, music, videos, news articles and other interactive media.</p>
